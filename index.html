How to run it well

Progression

When you can do 10 reps with good form on all 3 sets, add a small amount of weight next time.

Rest

Rest around 1–2 minutes for isolation exercises and 2–3 minutes for heavier compound lifts.

Balance

Keep one chest fly variation per session, add lateral or rear-delt work when shoulders need more balance.

Recovery

Use the two rest days properly. Stop a set when form breaks and avoid pushing through pain.
